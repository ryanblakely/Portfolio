A curated newsletter that helps readers discover new independent magazines from around the world.

## What It Does

Every week, Magfinder scans publishers and distributors to find newly released independent magazines, then delivers a curated selection straight to your inbox.

## Features

- **Weekly Curation** - Handpicked selections of the best new indie magazines
- **Global Coverage** - Magazines from publishers worldwide
- **Clean Reading Experience** - Minimal, focused design that highlights the content

## Technical Stack

Built with Next.js and TypeScript, deployed on Cloudflare Pages for fast global delivery.
