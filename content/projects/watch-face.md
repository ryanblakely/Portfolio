A clean, minimal watch face for Garmin devices.

## Design Philosophy

No clutter, no unnecessary complications. Just the time and essential information at a glance.

## Features

- **Battery Efficient** - Optimized to minimize power consumption
- **High Contrast** - Easy to read in any lighting condition
- **Essential Info Only** - Time, date, and battery level

Built with Monkey C, Garmin's programming language for wearables.
