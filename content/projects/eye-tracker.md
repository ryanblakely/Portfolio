An experimental app exploring eye tracking capabilities on Apple Vision Pro.

## What It Does

Uses the Vision Pro's built-in eye tracking to create interactive experiences driven by where you look.

## Technical Details

- **RealityKit Integration** - Leverages Apple's spatial computing framework
- **Real-time Tracking** - Responds instantly to eye movement
- **Native visionOS** - Built specifically for the Vision Pro platform

An exploration of what's possible with spatial computing and gaze-based interaction.
