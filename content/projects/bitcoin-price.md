A lightweight macOS menu bar app that displays the current Bitcoin price in real-time.

## Features

- **Live Price Updates** - Connects via WebSocket to receive instant price changes
- **Minimal Footprint** - Runs quietly in your menu bar without consuming resources
- **Clean Design** - Shows just the price, no clutter

## Technical Details

The app connects to a WebSocket endpoint to receive real-time price updates. This approach ensures you always see the current price without manual refreshing.

Built with SwiftUI for a native macOS experience that integrates seamlessly with your system.
